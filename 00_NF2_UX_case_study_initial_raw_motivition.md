1. log mental state
    - Why: paper say that NF2 patient easily have mental problem.
    
    - So what: 
        - Access professional mental support. 
        - Get peer support from others' stories. (3) medical data for research.

2. Manage Medical report for NF2 patients. 
    
    - Why: 
        - NF2 is a life-long chonic genetic disease. Patient should undergo annual examinations to minitor their health condition. So they have lots of reports should be managed well. 
        - It should be very careful for doctors to diagonose NF2 patient. Individual previous medical history is crucial.
        - Patient may visit doctors in different hospital, either countries or cities. Each insititution would have individual HIS(Health information system) to story patient infomation and eaxam reprot, and they are not always transperent to each other. Patient should summary and tell new doctor their condition. However, their narration may not always accurate, and include key points valuable for doctor. Doctors prefer to check the original report themselves.
    
    - So what: 
        - Digital medical reports folder, where patient can save all their report in the same place. Before visiting doctor. The app can summarize all patient's report as a timeline card. 

        > Note: (1) For medical, doctors won't rely on the evaluate result from AI, AI would better only use for "SUMMARIZE CURRENT CONTENT". (2) beside each record in timeline, it has a cite link like GM Notebook. To let reader quickly retrival original file.

3. Share the updated trustful news/research/peer posts related to NF2. e.g. Daily management, common symptoms - self check and prevent.
    - Why:
        - Chinese patient access less information compare to patients living overseas. They are eager to learn the lastest treatments, even they cannot afford them.
        - NF2 would result in various symptom guagually as the time goes by. When one notice an abnomal symptom, he/she may be in panic and is anxious that they want to know how to manage it.

4. Provide an option: Learn sign language
    - Why:
        - The most common symptom for NF2 is hearing loss. Normally patients would be diagnosed in their 20s, which mean post-deafness. Mostly they have no experience on sign language. In China, this will be more brutal, as there few official sign language resources to access.
