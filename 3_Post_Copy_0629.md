## Notes for posting

- **Lead as a patient, not a researcher.** That framing is doing the heavy lifting on trust.
- **Post mid-week, daytime US/EU** when groups are most active.
- **Don't cross-post the same text repeatedly** — Reddit flags duplicate self-promo. One post per community.
- **Engage in the comments** for the first day — replies and answered questions keep the post visible and signal good faith.
- **FB:** emoji is fine and on-tone for patient groups. **Reddit:** keep it plainer; over-formatting reads as marketing.
